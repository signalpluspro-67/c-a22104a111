Smart Flow is hunting for unusual options sweeps where the next-day open interest delta confirms the print was opening (real new positioning, not closing), filtered through EMA8/21 trend structure and a low-OI anomaly check. The thesis is mechanical: when a sweep buyer pays up across the ask and OI actually expands the next session, you're tagging along with someone who wanted size badly enough to telegraph it, and the EMA alignment keeps you from fading the tape. Score weights OI delta, premium dollars, signal persistence, and trend agreement, refreshed roughly every 30 minutes intraday.
