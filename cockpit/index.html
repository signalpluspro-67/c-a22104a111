The reason is mechanical. Passive flows and momentum strategies create a self-reinforcing loop. Index funds buy more of what is already big. Momentum strategies buy more of what is already going up. Passive funds sell what gets smaller. Momentum strategies sell what stops going up. The leaders catch down faster than the median can catch up.
