Long description (gros champ texte)

Description longue
Full description

The Philosopher Investor is the stock research desk I always wanted, now open to everyone.

Every night after the close, an engine reads the whole US market and runs a dozen tested setups on it: dip buys, breakouts, momentum leaders, squeezes, unusual options flow, names bottoming out. Real traders then go over what the machine flags and cut the noise. You wake up to a short, ranked board instead of the whole market.

Open any name and the story lands on one screen: the support and resistance that matter, a setup score and a fundamentals score out of 100, where the named funds are adding or trimming (from 13F filings, an inference), the options flow, and a plain read that leads with where the idea is wrong.

It also covers sectors, themes and social sentiment, plus a position manager that sizes each trade, sets alerts, and keeps a journal. It is paired with a newsletter I write myself.

Free sample and free newsletter. A membership unlocks the full app.
