ÉpictèteULTRA-LOW
Picks from: Swing Long · up to 1 position
The minimalist Stoic — even stricter than Sénèque. Holds AT MOST ONE position, and only the single highest-conviction proven-edge swing long (PULLBACK / BOTTOM). If nothing clears the bar, it sits in cash. Maximum patience, minimum exposure.
SénèqueLOW
Picks from: Swing Long · up to 2 positions
The Stoic — capital preservation first. Takes only the proven-edge swing longs (PULLBACK / BOTTOM, out-of-sample Sharpe about 1.8), stocks held for weeks. Waits for a clean quality setup and never chases. At most 2 positions.
AristoteMEDIUM
Picks from: Swing Long · up to 3 positions
The golden mean — diversified swing longs (PULLBACK / BOTTOM, both momentum and mean-reversion), balanced sizing. Up to 3 stock positions. Long-only, no chasing.
DiogèneHIGH
Picks from: Swing Long · Short · up to 3 positions
The provocateur — plays BOTH directions of the stock: rides proven swing longs but also takes counter-trend SHORT setups when the desk flags them. Up to 3 positions, long or short. Highest day-to-day swings.
HéracliteDEGEN
Picks from: Swing Long · Short · up to 4 positions
The pyromaniac — 'everything flows', rides the chaos. Most aggressive book: up to 4 concentrated long/short stock positions with NO diversification cap (can pile one direction). Biggest swings both ways. Spot only — no options, no leverage faked.
