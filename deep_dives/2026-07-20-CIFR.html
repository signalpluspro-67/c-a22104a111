The Big Picture

The technology sector is neutral and its momentum is dead. That is the environment you're walking into. Crypto is the active theme on the desk, and it is the only thread pulling capital toward names like this one.

The structural story is real. Bitcoin miners are pivoting into high-performance compute hosting for AI hyperscalers. The power infrastructure they already control is the asset. The demand for industrial-scale data centers is growing. The companies that can repurpose their sites fastest win.

This backdrop rewards a name that has already made the pivot. It punishes names that are still talking about it. You need concrete progress on HPC hosting, signed tenants, and operational power capacity. Everything else is a story.

Introducing CIFR

Cipher Digital Inc. develops and operates industrial-scale data centers for bitcoin mining and high-performance compute hosting in the United States. The company changed its name from Cipher Mining Inc. to Cipher Digital Inc. in February 2026. That rebrand signals the strategic shift. It wants to be known for digital infrastructure, not just crypto mining.

The company operates power at one bitcoin mining data center today. It maintains a pipeline across various sites for hyperscaler tenants. That pipeline is the bull case. If Cipher can convert pipeline into signed contracts, the revenue mix shifts away from pure crypto exposure toward contracted HPC hosting. Contracted revenue gets a different multiple than mining revenue.

Headquartered in New York with 66 employees, Cipher is small and lean. It was founded in 2021. You are buying a growth story, not a profitable business. The balance sheet and income statement tell you exactly what kind of risk you are taking.
