Introducing LODE

Comstock Inc. is a Nevada-based company commercializing technologies that extract, process, and convert under-utilized waste and natural resources into clean energy products. It operates through four segments: Fuels, Metals, Mining, and Strategic Investments.

The Fuels segment develops technology that converts lignocellulosic biomass into intermediates for refining into advanced renewable fuels. The Metals and Mining segments hold the Lucerne Project in Storey County, Nevada, and the Spring Valley Project in Lyon County, Nevada. The company also provides engineering and construction services and holds non-mining real estate, water rights, and securities investments through its Strategic Investments arm.

It is a technology commercialization story in early-stage materials, combining mining assets with clean-energy processing IP. The appeal is optionality. You own exposure to renewable fuels and precious metals in one equity, and the upside comes from technology breakthroughs or resource appreciation.
