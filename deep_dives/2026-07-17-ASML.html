Introducing ASML

ASML Holding is the only company on earth that makes extreme ultraviolet lithography systems. EUV is the machine that prints the smallest transistor features on advanced silicon wafers. Without ASML's machines, you do not get leading-edge chips from TSMC, Samsung, or Intel. That is the edge. It is a legal monopoly on the most critical step in semiconductor manufacturing.

The company sells two main product lines. EUV systems handle the most advanced nodes. Deep ultraviolet systems, or DUV, handle mature nodes and multi-patterning for less advanced chips. ASML also sells metrology and inspection systems to measure defect rates on wafers. The installed base of machines generates a growing services and upgrades revenue stream. That gives you a recurring layer on top of the cyclical equipment sales.

It operates globally with major customers concentrated in Taiwan, South Korea, the United States, and China. The stock trades as an ADR in the US with deep liquidity. This is a mega-cap technology stock with the liquidity of one. You can size into it without slippage concerns.
