Introducing RGEN

Repligen Corporation develops and commercializes bioprocessing technologies and systems. The company serves biopharmaceutical companies, contract manufacturing organizations, and laboratory researchers across North America, Europe, and the Asia Pacific. It was incorporated in 1981 and is headquartered in Waltham, Massachusetts.

The product portfolio spans filtration, chromatography, and process analytics. Its XCell ATF systems handle upstream cell retention. OPUS pre-packed chromatography columns handle purification. The PATsmart line covers real-time monitoring of bioprocess parameters. Protein A ligands serve as binding components in affinity chromatography resins.

The edge here is consumable density. Once a drugmaker designs Repligen hardware into its process, the recurring consumable revenue follows. That installed-base dynamic is what makes bioprocessing an attractive niche for long-duration exposure. You own a tools company that gets richer as more biologic drugs move through clinical and commercial production.
