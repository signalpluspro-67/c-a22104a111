Introducing MS

Morgan Stanley is a global financial holding company with three segments. Institutional Securities handles capital raising, M&A advisory, and trading. Wealth Management runs the advisor-led brokerage and planning business. Investment Management covers asset management across equities, fixed income, and alternatives.

It was founded in 1924.

The wealth management arm is the sticky revenue engine. Institutional Securities is the cyclical driver that swings with deal flow and trading volumes. Investment Management provides fee income with lower capital intensity. The blend gives Morgan Stanley diversified earnings, but the cyclical pieces tie it tightly to the financials cycle, which is currently EXHAUSTED.
