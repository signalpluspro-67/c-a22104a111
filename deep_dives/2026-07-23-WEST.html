Introducing WEST

Westrock Coffee Company is an integrated coffee, tea, flavors, extracts, and ingredients provider based in Little Rock, Arkansas.

The business operates through two segments. Beverage Solutions handles packaging, branded and private label coffee, single serve cups, ready-to-drink bottles and cans, and extract solutions for cold brew applications. Sustainable Sourcing and Traceability manages forward sales contracts for green coffee and the supply chain logistics around sourcing.

The edge here is integration. Westrock controls the path from green coffee sourcing through roasting, packaging, and distribution. That vertical structure matters when input costs move. A pure roaster eats the squeeze. A company with sourcing infrastructure, product development, and packaging under one roof can pass costs through or restructure its product mix. That is the argument for owning a coffee processor at this scale.
