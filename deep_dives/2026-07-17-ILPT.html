Introducing ILPT

Industrial Logistics Properties Trust is a real estate investment trust focused on owning and leasing industrial and logistics properties. The portfolio consists of 409 properties containing approximately 59.6 million rentable square feet across 39 states. Approximately 77% of annualized rental revenues come from investment grade tenants, subsidiaries of investment grade rated entities, or Hawaii land leases.

The company is managed by The RMR Group, an alternative asset management firm with over $37 billion in assets under management and 40 years of institutional experience in commercial real estate. That management relationship gives ILPT access to institutional-grade operational expertise that most small-cap REITs lack.

ILPT is headquartered in Newton, MA, and was incorporated in 2017 in Maryland. The company sits in the REIT Industrial subsector, which gives it direct exposure to the logistics and warehousing demand driven by e-commerce and supply chain restructuring. The portfolio is diversified geographically, but the Hawaii land leases add a unique revenue stream that most industrial REITs cannot match.
