Introducing HTLD

Heartland Express is a dry van truckload carrier operating across the United States, Mexico, and Canada. The company runs short, medium, and long-haul routes under the Heartland Express, Millis Transfer, Smith Transport, and CFI brands. It serves retailers, manufacturers, and parcel carriers in consumer goods, food products, and automotive industries.

It is headquartered in North Liberty, Iowa, and was founded in 1978. Heartland built its reputation on a debt-free balance sheet and disciplined cost management. That edge matters in a freight recession when overleveraged competitors are forced to park trucks.

The company has acquired several carriers over the years to expand its network. Those deals added capacity and geographic reach. They also added integration costs that weigh on current margins. You are buying a consolidator in a consolidating industry.
