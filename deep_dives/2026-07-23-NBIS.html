Introducing NBIS

Nebius Group is an AI infrastructure company built on the remains of Yandex. The company changed its name in August 2024 and pivoted hard into full-stack AI computing. It builds large-scale GPU clusters, cloud platforms, and developer tools. It has a strategic alliance with NVIDIA to support the expansion of its AI cloud infrastructure. That NVIDIA relationship is the edge. It gives Nebius access to the GPUs that every AI developer needs and cannot easily get.

The company operates 3 segments. The core AI cloud business is the revenue engine. TripleTen is an edtech platform that re-skills people for technology careers. Avride develops autonomous driving technology for self-driving cars and delivery robotics. The AI cloud segment is where the growth and the valuation live. The other two are optionality.

It serves the United States, the United Kingdom, and international markets. The company was founded in 1989, but the current incarnation is barely 2 years old. You are buying a post-restructuring AI pure-play with a clean strategic focus and a real partnership with the dominant chipmaker in the world.
