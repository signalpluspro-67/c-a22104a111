Introducing ELV

Elevance Health is one of the largest health-benefits companies in the United States. It operates under the Anthem Blue Cross and Blue Shield, Wellpoint, and Carelon brands, serving individual, employer group, Medicare, Medicaid, and Federal Employee Program members. The company runs four segments: Health Benefits, CarelonRx, Carelon Services, and Corporate & Other.

Beyond insurance premiums, Elevance has built out Carelon as a services and pharmacy-benefits platform. CarelonRx handles pharmacy services including home delivery, specialty pharmacies, claims adjudication, and formulary management. Carelon Services covers behavioral health, palliative care, virtual care, payment integrity, and clinical analytics. This diversification gives Elevance revenue from both risk-based insurance and fee-based services.

The company was formerly known as Anthem and rebranded as Elevance Health in June 2022.
