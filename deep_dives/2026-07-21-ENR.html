Introducing ENR

Energizer Holdings makes and sells batteries and auto-care products worldwide. You know the brands. Energizer, Eveready, Rayovac, and Varta cover household and specialty batteries. Armor All, STP, and A/C PRO cover the automotive care side. The company sells through every channel you can think of, from warehouse clubs to e-commerce to military stores.
The battery business is mature and cash-generative. People buy batteries in recessions and expansions alike. The auto-care portfolio adds diversification and shelf space at auto parts retailers. Energizer is headquartered in Saint Louis, Missouri, and employs roughly 6,050 people.
The company carries significant debt from its acquisition history. That leverage shapes the entire investment case. It amplifies the earnings power on the way up and the risk on the way down. You are buying a consumer staples business mislabeled as Industrials, trading at a deep value multiple with real balance-sheet risk attached.
