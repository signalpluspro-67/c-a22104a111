Introducing $EBAY

eBay runs marketplace platforms that connect buyers and sellers across the United States, the United Kingdom, China, Germany, and beyond. Founded in 1995 and headquartered in San Jose, the business spans ebay.com, a set of off-platform operations, and the eBay mobile apps.

The edge is the focused-category strategy. Trading cards, authenticated sneakers and luxury goods, refurbished tech, and motor parts are categories where eBay's depth of inventory and trust tools are hard to copy. These are higher-value, higher-margin transactions than generic retail, and they keep dedicated buyers coming back.

Depop adds a Gen-Z resale audience to the mix, extending eBay's reach into fashion resale where younger users already shop. The throughline is a marketplace that takes a cut of every sale, carries almost no inventory risk itself, and converts that into steady cash.
