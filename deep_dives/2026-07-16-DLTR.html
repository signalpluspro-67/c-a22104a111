Introducing DLTR

Dollar Tree operates 15,000 stores under the Dollar Tree and Dollar Tree Canada brands. The company sells everyday consumables, variety merchandise, and seasonal goods. It was founded in 1986 and is based in Chesapeake, Virginia.

The business has three legs. Consumables drive the most traffic: household paper, chemicals, food, candy, health, personal care, and frozen and refrigerated items. Variety merchandise covers toys, housewares, gifts, stationery, party goods, and softlines. Seasonal goods anchor the holiday calendar with Christmas, Easter, Halloween, and Valentine's Day.

The edge is scale plus the price point. At $1.2 price-to-sales, you are buying a discount giant at a thin multiple. Dollar Tree serves the consumer who needs to stretch every dollar, and that consumer base grows when the economy softens.
