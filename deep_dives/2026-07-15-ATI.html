Introducing ATI

ATI Inc. produces specialty materials and complex components worldwide. The company operates two segments. High Performance Materials & Components handles titanium, nickel-based superalloys, and precision forgings for aerospace and defense. Advanced Alloys & Solutions handles zirconium, specialty alloys, and flat-rolled products for energy, medical, and industrial markets.

The edge is in the metallurgy. ATI produces metallic powder alloys and advanced materials that go into jet engines, medical implants, and nuclear reactors. These are high-spec products with long qualification cycles. Once a customer designs an engine around your titanium alloy, switching costs are enormous.

The company serves aerospace and defense, medical and specialty energy, oil and gas, and automotive markets. It was formerly known as Allegheny Technologies Incorporated.
