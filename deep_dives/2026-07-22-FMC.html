Introducing FMC

FMC Corporation is an agricultural sciences company founded in 1883 and headquartered in Philadelphia. The company develops, markets, and sells crop protection chemicals globally. Its product portfolio includes insecticides, herbicides, fungicides, biologicals, crop nutrition, and seed treatment products.

FMC operates in Latin America, North America, Europe, the Middle East, Africa, and Asia. It sells through its own sales organization, alliance partners, independent distributors, and sales representatives.

The edge here is scale and portfolio breadth in crop protection. FMC is a established player with a global distribution network. The problem is that the balance sheet is stressed, margins have collapsed, and the stock has been punished accordingly. You are looking at a company in survival mode, priced for distress.
