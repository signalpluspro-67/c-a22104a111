Introducing JEF

Jefferies Financial Group is a middle-market investment bank. It operates in two segments. Investment Banking and Capital Markets handles advisory work for mergers and acquisitions, debt underwriting, and equity placement. Asset Management oversees a portfolio of alternative investment platforms. The company serves public and private companies, institutional investors, and government entities.

The firm has built its edge by targeting the middle market. The mega-deals go to Goldman Sachs and Morgan Stanley. Jefferies competes for the deals below that tier, where the client base is large and the advisory fees are substantial. It has grown its equities research and trading desk into a credible counterweight to the bulge bracket.

The company was formerly known as Leucadia National Corporation. It changed its name in May 2018 to reflect its identity as a pure-play investment bank.
