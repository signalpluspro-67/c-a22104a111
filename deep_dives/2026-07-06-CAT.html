The Big Picture

The AI trade has moved from the chips to the electricity that runs them, and Caterpillar builds the hardware behind that power. Its stock is up roughly 73% year to date on that shift.

Data centers need generators, engines and on-site power, and CAT's Energy & Transportation segment sells reciprocating engines, generator sets and gas turbines straight into that demand. When a hyperscaler needs standby and prime power for a new campus, the order flows to a short list of suppliers, and Caterpillar is at the top of it. That is the structural story driving the multiple.

That story has a second edge. The industrials group reads exhausted here, deep into a late chase where the easy money is already booked. Beta on CAT is 1.56, so it moves more than the market in both directions. When a theme runs this far this fast, the reward for chasing the last leg shrinks and the risk of a sharp reversal grows.

This backdrop rewards a name with real cash flows and a real seat at the AI-power table, bought at a sane level rather than at the highs. Caterpillar is that name. The discipline is in the entry.

Introducing $CAT

Caterpillar Inc. You know the yellow machines. The business underneath them is broader than the brand.

Four engines drive the company. Construction Industries sells excavators, loaders, graders and the parts that keep them running. Resource Industries builds the mining trucks, shovels, drills and autonomous fleet technology that pull ore out of the ground. Energy & Transportation makes the reciprocating engines, generator sets, gas compressors and locomotive components, and this is the segment the market now prices as an AI-power proxy. Financial Products is the captive finance arm that lends against all of it.

The edge is scale and the dealer network. Caterpillar's distribution and aftermarket parts business gives it a recurring, higher-margin revenue stream that pure equipment makers cannot match. That installed base is the moat. The power-generation demand from data centers is the new growth layer sitting on top of it.
