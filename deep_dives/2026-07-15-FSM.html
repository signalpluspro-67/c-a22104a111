Introducing FSM

Fortuna Mining Corp. is a Vancouver-based precious metals producer with operations across 5 countries: Argentina, Côte d'Ivoire, Mexico, Peru, and Senegal. The company runs the Lindero gold mine, the Séguéla gold mine, and the Caylloma silver, lead, and zinc mine. It rebranded from Fortuna Silver Mines to Fortuna Mining Corp. in June 2024 to reflect its increasingly gold-weighted portfolio.

The operating segments are Mansfield (Lindero), Sango (Séguéla), and Bateas (Caylloma). Gold is now the dominant revenue driver, but the Caylloma asset keeps silver and base metals in the mix. That gives FSM exposure to both precious metals, which matters when you are playing a bounce tied to firm silver prices.

Fortuna is a mid-tier producer, small enough to move on sector flows but large enough to generate real cash. Its market cap sits near $2.63 billion with $1.09 billion in trailing revenue. This is a producing miner, a junior explorer.
