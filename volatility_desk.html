VIX and the term-structure regime

VIX spot (candles) and the VIX / VIX3M term-structure ratio over the last months. Below the 1.0 line is contango, the normal calm state. Above it is backwardation, the front-month stress tell.

How to read
Contango (ratio below 1.0) is the normal regime: near-term vol is cheaper than 3-month, the market is calm. Backwardation (ratio above 1.0) means traders are paying up for immediate protection, the classic front-end stress signal that often precedes a spike.
