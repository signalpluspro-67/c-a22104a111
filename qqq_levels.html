Dealer gamma (GEX walls)

Dealer gamma is how option market-makers must hedge. Into the call wall they sell strength and buy weakness, which pins price and caps upside; lose the put wall and that hedging flips, so moves accelerate. Read the call wall as a ceiling / magnet, and the put wall as the floor that, once broken, opens air below.
