How to read
A radar of option positioning and implied vol on the macro and commodity ETFs. It shows where the market is paying up for a move, which often precedes the move, but it is not a forecast. IV-rank is ranked over the available history (about three months of stored implied vol), not a full year. UNG, IEF and XME have no options chain in our data yet, so their vol and skew show n/a. Options chain and IV are end of day 2026-06-26, flow is end of day 2026-06-25.
