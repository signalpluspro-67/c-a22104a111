Divergence signature, pairs that must confirm each other

These pairs must confirm each other. When one rolls over while the other keeps climbing, that gap, the "jaws", is the early divergence signal. Each chart shows the ratio with its Bollinger bands and magnet levels, same series as the archived Kerberos dashboard.

DJT / DJI

Last 0.4182 · Z +0.46 · Normal range
Dow Theory, Transports must confirm Industrials

Dow Transports divided by Dow Industrials. When DJT diverges down while DJI prints a new high, the bull market is fragile. The classic Dow Theory tell: if transports do not follow industrials, distribution is underway.

AI read🚨 Transports are running far ahead of their usual pace versus the industrials, sitting near the top of where they've traded all year , a once-a-year kind of stretch, and today's jump (+1.7%) pushed it even further. When one part of the market gets this stretched above its normal range, it usually drifts back toward average, so watch whether transports start cooling off or the industrials catch up to confirm the move is real.

AUDJPY vs SPY

Last 0.1515 · Z -1.30 · Normal range
Risk-On thermometer must confirm the S&P

AUD/JPY (the carry-trade risk gauge) divided by SPY. AUDJPY marked the big pre-crashes since 2000 (Volmageddon, COVID). When AUDJPY breaks down while the index keeps rising, that gap is the warning.

AI read⚠️ The carry-trade gauge is sliding while stocks hold up, the same quiet split that flashed before past market scares , and it's now sitting a bit below its normal range despite today's bounce. Watch whether it keeps drifting lower over the next week or two; a steady fall while the S&P stays high is the warning sign, while a snap back up means the worry fades.

CHFEUR

Last 1.0797 · Z -1.85 · Normal range
Swiss Franc vs Euro, safe-haven flow

Swiss Franc against the Euro. A rising CHFEUR is European risk-off money moving to safety, roughly 55% correlated with VIX over 100 days. The early tell that the continent is getting scared.

AI read👀 The Swiss Franc just slipped a bit against the Euro and is sitting just under its normal range, so Europe's nervous-money gauge is leaning calm right now rather than flashing fear. Watch for it to turn back up: a steady climb in the Franc is the early tell that European investors are getting scared, and it tends to show up alongside a jump in the overall market fear gauge.

GLD / SPY

Last 0.5184 · Z -1.80 · Normal range
Gold vs Equity, real haven flow

Gold divided by SPY. In a true risk-off, gold rises while the index falls so the ratio explodes. If only the index drops and gold stays flat, it is a vol pop without haven flow, a fake risk-off.

AI read🚨 Gold versus stocks has been crushed down to one of its lowest readings of the past few months , gold is badly lagging the stock market, the opposite of what you'd see if investors were running for safety. Watch whether this ratio starts climbing: a sustained turn up would be the first real sign that money is rotating out of stocks and into the classic safe haven.

KRE / XLF

Last 1.3388 · Z +0.26 · Normal range
Regional banks vs big banks, credit health

Regional banks against the big financials. When KRE breaks down hard versus XLF, regional credit is stressing (the SVB-style 2023 setup), a very early credit-crunch tell that the mega-banks mask.

AI read👀 Regional banks are quietly outpacing the big banks right now, sitting a bit above their usual range for the past few months , the opposite of the stress you'd see in a credit scare, so no alarm here. The thing to watch is a sudden sharp reversal: if regional banks start dropping hard against the big banks, that's often the earliest hint of trouble in the lending system before it shows up anywhere else.

QQQ / SPY

Last 0.9918 · Z +2.05 · Stretched high
Tech leadership must confirm the broad rally

Nasdaq 100 over the S&P 500. A z above +2 is extreme tech euphoria. If the index keeps rising but QQQ rolls over, breadth is dying under the surface. Confluence with SMH/SPY double-confirms leadership exhaustion.

AI read🚨 Tech is stretched to an unusually high level versus the broad market , this is the kind of extreme tech-over-everything reading that shows up only rarely and tends to snap back toward normal. Watch whether the broad market keeps climbing while tech stalls or rolls over: if the two start splitting apart, it's a warning that the tech leadership powering this rally is running out of gas.

BTC / SPY

Last 84.2185 · Z -2.07 · Stretched low
Crypto leading equity, the 24/7 risk gauge

Bitcoin over SPY. Crypto trades around the clock, driven by specs and retail, so it tends to lead. When BTC cracks ahead of the index, that is an early risk-off warning. When BTC rips while the index is flat, liquidity is coming.

AI read🚨 Bitcoin has dropped well below its usual level versus the S&P 500 , a once-in-a-while low reading , and since crypto often wobbles a week or two before stocks do, this is an early caution flag. Watch whether stocks start to soften over the next week or two, or whether Bitcoin snaps back, which would clear the warning.

RSP / QQQ

Last 0.2835 · Z -1.51 · Normal range
Equal-weight vs Tech, concentration check

Equal-weighted S&P divided by the Nasdaq 100. When QQQ crushes RSP, the mega-caps dominate and breadth fades. A z below -1.5 flags extreme concentration in a handful of names.

AI read👀 The equal-weight S&P is slipping versus the Nasdaq giants again , down nearly 2% today and sitting a bit below where it's traded over the last few months, meaning a handful of mega-cap tech names are pulling the market on their own. Watch whether this keeps sliding toward a once-or-twice-a-year extreme (which often snaps back as the average stock catches up) or stabilizes here; a turn back up would signal the rally is broadening out to more companies.
