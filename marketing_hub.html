Growth actions ~15 min/day, ranked by impact

Do the HIGH ones every day - they compound. The rest are weekly. Everything here is low-effort on your side by design.
Recommend 3 peers a dayHIGH5 min
Open the Peer reach-out list and recommend 3 names you do not yet. Most reciprocate within a week - this is the single fastest compounding lever at your size.
Send 2 swap DMs a dayHIGH5 min
Message 2 peers (use the 'message ->' link - it opens their profile). One line: 'Been recommending you, love your work - want to swap recommendations?' Track replies with the status chip.
Post 1 Substack Note dailyHIGH5 min
Drop one chart or one sharp line from your latest issue as a Note. Notes are the #1 in-app discovery engine right now - more reach than the post itself.
Pin your best postHIGH2 min
Set your top performer as the featured post so first-time visitors land on your strongest work. Set once, compounds forever.
Reply to every commentMED10 min
Answer all open comments on your last 2 posts. Engaged commenters convert to paid at several times the base rate.
Welcome new subscribersMED10 min
Send a short note to subs from the last 7 days asking what they want more of. Direct signal plus goodwill, and it surfaces paid intent.
Cross-post to one X threadMED10 min
Turn this week's thesis into a 4-tweet thread with the chart, link in the last tweet. Repurpose, do not rewrite.
Resurface an evergreenMED2 min
Link an older high-open post inside your next issue. Free traffic to proven work, zero new writing.
Refresh your About pageLOW10 min
First two lines must state who you help and the one promise of the newsletter. Drives the subscribe decision.
Ask for one testimonialLOW5 min
DM a happy paid sub for a one-line testimonial, put it on your About page. Social proof lifts free-to-paid.
